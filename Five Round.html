Five Rounds Package

Enjoy a luxury stay with premium golfing experiences.

The Five Rounds Package is the ultimate experience for golf enthusiasts seeking luxury and excellence. This package includes a five-night full-board stay in a beautifully appointed luxury suite, offering unmatched comfort and amenities. Golfers can enjoy five rounds of 18-hole play, providing ample opportunity to perfect their game amidst stunning landscapes. A highlight of the package is one guaranteed round on the renowned Sea View course celebrated for its breathtaking ocean views and challenging layout.
