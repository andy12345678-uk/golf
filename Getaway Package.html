Getaway Break Package

Short stays with plenty of golf options to enjoy.

What’s Included:

    The **Getaway Break Package** is a perfect option for a quick, relaxing escape. It includes an overnight stay with a full breakfast, dinner at one of the resort's restaurants, and access to golf courses (with additional charges for premium courses). Guests also enjoy complimentary club hire, ensuring a hassle-free golfing experience. This package offers great value for those looking to unwind while enjoying the resort’s amenities and activities, making it an ideal choice for a short getaway filled with both relaxation and sport.
