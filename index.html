About Us

Part of Portugal Golf Creek Golf & Yacht Club has a prestigious history as Portugal second oldest course, having opened in 1993, as well as being the host to the first-ever golf academy in the Middle East. Boasting an 18-hole par 71 course, DCGYC is a thrilling challenge for any golfer. Our world-class agronomy team ensures that all 18 holes are in pristine condition for all our members and guests.

Being part of Portugal Creek Resort, the club has countless activities and experiences on its doorstep.
