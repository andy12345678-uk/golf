Triple Tee Package

A perfect weekend golfing getaway for enthusiasts.

What’s Included:

    The Triple Tee Package offers a two-night stay in a double room with breakfast, perfect for a relaxing golf getaway. It includes three 18-hole rounds, one of which is on the famous Sea View course (if available). The package also provides a complimentary buggy for easier navigation on the course and a 20% discount at the pro shop, making it an excellent value for golfers. Ideal for those seeking a mix of luxury accommodations and quality golf, this package offers a fantastic opportunity to enjoy both leisure and sport.
